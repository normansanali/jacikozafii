Who is Lucas Glovers caddie Don Cooper?

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Don Cooper is the caddie of former U.S. Open champion and professional golfer Lucas Glover. The two have been working together for two decades now. They started working together in 2003 when Lucas officially hired him.

In 2009, Cooper was on the bag for Glover when he won his first major, the U.S. Open at Bethpage.

Many, especially fans of Lucas have wished to know almost everything about Cooper since he is the caddie of their player. In this article, we will dove into his personal life to know his family and early life.

Who is Lucas Glover’s caddie Don Cooper?

As previously stated, Don Cooper is the caddie of Pro golfer, Lucas Glover. The two have known each other since the late 1990s/early 2000s. This was when Lucas was in college at Clemson University. At the time, Cooper was a club caddie at Augusta National Golf Club.

Talking about his early life and family. there is no information about that on the internet since he has decided to keep his details away from the general public.

It is, however, believed that he is a family man who is married with at least a child as of now( we are yet to confirm that).
