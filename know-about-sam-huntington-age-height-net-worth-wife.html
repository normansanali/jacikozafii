Know About Sam Huntington; Age, Height, Net Worth, Wife

Publish date: 2024-07-25
Published Date: 12 Feb, 2019 Updated date: 12 Feb, 2019 By Celebs Podium

Acclaimed as a prolific TV actor, Sam Huntington has been a part of more than 3 dozen TV shows and movies which has strengthened his professional wealth. Besides, the New Hampshire resident is on cloud nine amidst the married life with actress wife, Rachael Klein.

Sam’s entertainment journey got underway in 1996 with his part in the TV movie, Harvest of Fire. Gradually, the unknown actor became famed with a series of movies and TV shows up his belt. He is known among the fans for his role as Josh Levison, a werewolf in the Syfy series, Being Human.

Short Bio: Age, Height

Sam Huntington, was born on 1 April 1982, in Peterborough, New Hampshire, the U.S. His mother, Christine Stabile owned and operated the Black Box Theatre.

The 36-years-of age American actor stands at a tall height of 1.78m. Besides, he carries American nationality and possesses white ethnicity.

Career, Net Worth & Earnings

The proclaimed TV and movie star bags a commendable net worth of $2 Million. All of his wealth is the result of Sam’s bright acting career. Since stepping his foot in the entertainment business with Harvest of Fire (1996), Sam went on to widen his acting credentials with Detroit Rock City (1999), Not Another Teen Movie (2001), and Rolling Kansas (2003). Besides that, he also appeared as Jimmy Olsen in Bryan Singer’s 2006 superhero-movie Superman Returns.

Sam Huntington during his fourth season on Being Human (Picture Source: Sam’s Official Website)

Apparently, Sam’s TV career came to limelight when he was cast as Josh Levison, a werewolf in the Syfy series, Being Human in 2011. He was a series regular till the end episode of the show which aired in April 2014. Sam’s most recent TV appearance was in one of the episodes of 2018 American series iZombie.  

Long-Haul Married Life With Actress Wife, Rachael Klein

Sam Huntington is a married man. The being human star shared the wedding vows with his then-girlfriend-of-five-years, Rachel Klein in 2006. Rachel, who is currently on her late 30s, is a two-time Primetime Emmy-nominated actress.

After sharing a moment together for the first time in 2001, Sam and Rachel went on to work together in the 2003 comedy movie Rolling Kansas. Subsequently, their closeness got a new name of husband and wife on 12 August 2006.

Sam and his wife, Rachel celebrated their 12th wedding anniversary in August 2018 (Picture Source: Sam’s Instagram)

Since tying the wedding knots, the star couple has welcomed two children in their lives. Their first-born, a son named Charlie Huntington came to this world in 2009. Three years later, Sam and Rachel welcomed a beautiful daughter, whom they named Audrey.

Sam is doing so well with his relationship life. In fact, he has found a perfect partner to hold in hands in every thick and thin. At this point, the family resides in Los Angeles.

Some Interesting Facts About Sam Huntington

  • Sam initiated his acting career at the Black Box Theatre, owned and operated by his mother, Christine Stabile.
  • His great-uncle is a former actor, Ralph Bellamy, who died of a lung ailment on 29 November 1991.
