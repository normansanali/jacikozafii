Early Life and Career Beginnings

Kelly Bishop was born in Colorado Springs, Colorado, and raised in Denver. She began her career as a ballet dancer before transitioning into acting. Her first major role was in the Broadway production of “A Chorus Line”, for which she won a Tony Award in 1976.
