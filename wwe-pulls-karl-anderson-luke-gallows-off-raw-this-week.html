WWE Pulls Karl Anderson &amp; Luke Gallows Off RAW This Week

Publish date: 2024-09-16

WWE has a lot of plans in motion on the road to WrestleMania, but that doesn’t mean that plans can’t change. The company had two popular Superstars booked for this week’s show, but then that appearance was nixed for some reason.

Sean Sapp reported a few notes about tonight’s episode of WWE RAW. You can click here for spoiler on one segment that involves a “Missing” Superstar.

It was further reported that the OC were booked for RAW at one time. Then Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows were pulled from the show for some reason. It was not noted how they were slated to be used.

Speaking of changes, OC were promoted for Raw, and at one point this weekend that was also nixed. They aren’t listed on the promoted Raw Rundown

The OC have very unique deal as part of their new WWE contracts. Karl Anderson and Luke Gallows don’t have to travel to shows that they’re not booked on. That means, since they’re not booked on the show, they won’t make the trip to RAW this week.

We will have to see when Luke Gallows and Karl Anderson appear next, but AJ Styles’ absence certainly put a hamper on their momentum. Keep checking back with Ringside News as we continue covering this, and every other story in the pro wrestling world.
