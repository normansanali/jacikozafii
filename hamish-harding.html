Hamish Harding Career

Harding pursued a degree in Chemical Engineering from Cambridge University, where he gained valuable knowledge and skills in the field. Following his graduation, he joined Logica, a prominent UK IT consultancy company, in 1988.

Over the course of 14 years, from 1988 to 2002, he held various IT technical and managerial roles within the company. Notably, Harding served as the Managing Director at Logica India from 1997 to 2002, where he successfully led the organization’s operations in the country.

In 2003, driven by his entrepreneurial spirit, Hamish Harding founded his own company, Action Group. Under his leadership, Action Aviation, a subsidiary of Action Group, emerged as a renowned aircraft brokerage firm with a strong presence in the Middle East and Asia.

In 2015 alone, the company achieved remarkable sales of over US$200 million in pre-owned business jets. Moreover, Action Aviation has recently facilitated the sale of several business jets to Asian buyers, including a Boeing Business Jet and a Gulfstream G650ER.
