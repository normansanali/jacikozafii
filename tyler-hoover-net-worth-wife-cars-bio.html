What is Tyler Hoover’s net worth? Who is Tyler Hoover’s wife?

Introduction

Tyler Hoover is an American YouTuber who runs the ”Hoovie’s Garage” YouTube channel.

His channel features videos where Tyler purchases unusual or broken cars and then films how he fixes them.

Sometimes, Hoover sells cars for a loss since he spends too much money on repairs or modifications. 

Biography

Tyler was born in the USA. He grew up in Wichita, Kansas.

He graduated from Andover High School in 2005.

From January 2007 to January 2009, Tyler worked as a used car salesman at Joe Self BMW.

Hoover earned a bachelor’s degree in Political Science and Government from Wichita State University.

From January 2010 to January 2015, Tyler was the owner of Ad Astra Automotive.

He also worked as an opening coordinator and operations support for FFC Midwest as well as a freelance automotive writer, especially for AutoTrader Oversteer.
