Brady Noon is an American actor and television personality who was born on December 30, 2005.

His portrayal of Tommy Darmody in the HBO crime thriller “Boardwalk Empire” propelled him to fame.

After he appeared in “The Mighty Ducks: Game Changers,” a Disney+ streaming series, the young actor’s fame increased in 2021.

Together with his parents and siblings, he continues to reside in Ocean County, New Jersey. Noon made 30 appearances in “Boardwalk Empire” episodes between 2010 and 2013.

The young actor also has a sizable fan base that follows him on his social media channels. The young man’s Instagram account has more than 50,000 followers.

Noon has a twin brother named Conor Noon and an elder sister named Madison Noon. His parents are unknown.
