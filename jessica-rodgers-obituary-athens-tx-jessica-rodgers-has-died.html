Jessica Rodgers Obituary, Death – In Chicago, Illinois, on October 25, 2021, Jessica Nicole Rodgers, age 28 and a resident of Zion, Illinois, passed away while surrounded by her family. She is the daughter of Daniel Rodgers, who is married to Mary Louise Rohan, and she was born on June 14, 1993 in Maywood, Illinois.

In addition to being a member of New Season Community Church in Zion, Illinois, Jessica received her high school diploma from Zion Benton Township High School. Besides drawing, Jessica enjoyed spending time in the great outdoors, particularly when she was camping, boating, skateboarding, or riding her bike. She also enjoyed swimming.

Jessica is survived by her father, Daniel W. Rodgers; her aunts, Lisa Rodgers, Tina (Brian Rasmussen) Polek, and Pearl (Jim) Hessmer; her uncles, Walter Rodgers, Richard Rodgers, Adam Rodgers, Brian Rodgers, Michael Steinkopf, Daniel (Lucy) Rohan, and Michael Rohan; her cousins, Kara, Heather, Megan, Crystal, Melissa, Eric, Madeline, Cassandra, Jenny, Chrissy, Josh, Ben, Carl, Britney, Steven, Kathernie, and Allison; her boyfriend, Travis; and her best friends, Alyssa, Danielle, Britney, Lexsy, and Ashleigh are among those who miss her.

Mary, Jessica’s mother, as well as her grandparents, William Steinkopf and Daniel Rohan, both of whom were married to Patricia, and an aunt named Sheila Rodgers, all passed away before Jessica reached adulthood. There will be a visitation on Saturday, November 6, 2021 from three o’clock until five o’clock, and then a funeral service will take place at five o’clock at the Congdon Funeral Home, which is located at 3012 Sheridan Road in Zion, Illinois 60099.
