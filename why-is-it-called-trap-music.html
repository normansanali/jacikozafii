Why Is It Called Trap Music?

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Why is it called Trap Music? It is called Trap Music because it once described hip-hop as associated with illegal substance dealers.

The trap is a slang word that is used in Atlanta to define a place or a house where the dealers would go to buy or sell illegal substances. There was a heightened risk of them being caught by the police, but still, the dealers would go there to procure their goods.

The term comes from the phrase “mouse trap.” Just like how a mouse walks into the mouse trap to get food, the dealers would walk into the trap to make a deal with a high chance of being trapped and caught by the cops.

What is Trap Music? Some Examples

Trap music is a sub-genre of hip-hop that originated in the early 1990s in Atlanta, Georgia. It uses snare drums and hi-hats for its signature sound.

Other instruments in trap music which help to characterize the genre are 808 kick drums, a drum machine, synthesizers, samplers, and of course the rapping.

Trap Music songs are 1. Drip Too Hard 2. Pushin P 3. Codeine Crazy. 

Some other examples of Trap Music are:

  • Publicity Stunt
  • Bad & Boujee
  • Runnin
  • No Mediocre
  • Trap Queen

What Does Trap Music Mean? Definition And Meaning

Trap music means rap about a lifestyle based on dealings of illegal substances and medications.

The meaning behind the name of this genre originates from the Trap house where the dealings for illegal substances are done.

Trap Music Origin

Trap music originated during the 1990s and early 2000s. It comes from the poor neighborhoods around the urban areas of Atlanta in Georgia.

Who Owns The Trap Music Museum?

The trap music museum in Atlanta, Georgia is owned by T.I. He is a famous Atlanta native rapper.

The museum is co-owned by his business associate William Sparks.

Where Did Trap Music Start?

Trap music started in Atlanta, Georgia. The Southern US state is the birthplace of the genre.

The emergence of rap music from the ghettos and into mainstream popularity helped inspire a new generation which henceforth brought new artists into the hip-hop scene during the 1980s.

Inspired by the hip-hop culture and its precedent, many new artists during the 1990s such as UGK, Tupac, Biggie, and NWK among others birthed what now is known as gangsta rap.

The contents of gangsta rap included a violent lifestyle and the underlooked and underdeveloped neighborhoods in the urban areas of the US. Gangsta rap would capture the imagination of the poor neighborhoods of Atlanta, Georgia, which then became a catalyst in the emergence of Trap music.

Who Is The King Of Trap Music?

The King of Trap Music is hard to simply state. Everyone has their own opinions on who should be considered the king.

Many people believe Gucci Mane to be the greatest Trap artist of all time while others might side with T.I.

In an article by Complex from 2018, they give their flowers to both Gucci and T.I. but proclaim Shawty Redd as the most crucial figure in the development of Trap music as it was he who originated the signature sound used in the genre.

Reddit Poll To Decide Trap Music King

A poll was conducted in a Reddit community named RAP with 878k members in 2022. It was to declare who the true king of trap music was.

120 people voted, and among them:

  • 37 selected Future
  • 30 who sided with Travis Scott.
  • Gucci Mane and T.I. both received 17 votes each
