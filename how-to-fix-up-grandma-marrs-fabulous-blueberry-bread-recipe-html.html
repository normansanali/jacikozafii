How to Fix up Grandma Marrs Fabulous blueberry bread recipe

Publish date: 2024-07-14

While the exact blueberry bread recipe from Grandma Marrs featured on “Fixer to Fabulous” may not have been published, you can create a similar blueberry bread using a standard recipe. Here’s a basic blueberry bread recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 ripe bananas
  • 1 ½ cups fresh blueberries (130g)
  • ½ cup plain yogurt (45g)
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour (130g)
  • ¾ cup brown sugar (150g) or ⅓ cup honey (80ml)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (45g)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour a loaf pan.
  • In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until they are smooth.
  • Add the plain yogurt, brown sugar (or honey), eggs, vanilla extract, and melted butter to the mashed bananas. Mix well to combine.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, salt, and baking soda.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  • Gently fold in the fresh blueberries.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Remove the blueberry bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10-15 minutes.
  • After it has cooled slightly, remove the bread from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack.

    • Feel free to make substitutions based on your preferences or dietary restrictions, such as using honey instead of brown sugar, whole wheat flour instead of all-purpose flour, or vanilla yogurt instead of plain yogurt. Enjoy your homemade blueberry bread, inspired by Grandma Marrs!
