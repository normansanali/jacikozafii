When and where is the Europa League final 2024?

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The UEFA Europa League is one of the most prestigious tournaments in European football, featuring top clubs from across the continent.

The 2023/24 season of the Europa League has concluded with the highly anticipated final, where two determined contenders battled for the ultimate glory.

Courtesy of Fan Arch, here's a detailed overview of the Europa League final in 2024, including information about the date, time, location, and other significant details related to the event.

Date and Time

The 2024 UEFA Europa League final took place on the 22nd of May 2024.

The final match at the Dublin Arena in the Republic of Ireland promised an exciting and hotly contested competition, following in the footsteps of previous thrilling finals.

In the event of a tied score at the end of normal time, two 15-minute periods of extra time would be played. If the tie persisted, the winner would be determined by a penalty shoot-out.

Location

The final of the 2023/24 UEFA Europa League was hosted at the Dublin Arena, also known as the Aviva Stadium, in Dublin, Ireland.

This marked the sixth different country in six years to host the Europa League final, showcasing the tournament's dynamic nature and its appeal to fans from various regions.

The choice of Dublin as the venue provided fans with the opportunity for a potential short flight or ferry trip to the Irish capital, adding to the accessibility and excitement surrounding the event.

The Dublin Arena, home to the Republic of Ireland's national football and Ireland's rugby union team, had previously hosted the Europa League final in 2011, during which FC Porto emerged victorious against Sporting Braga.

The stadium's historical significance in European football further added to the allure of hosting the final once again in 2024.

The decision to award hosting rights to Dublin was a result of a settlement agreement by UEFA to recognize the efforts and financial investment made to host UEFA Euro 2020, underscoring the city's commitment to promoting and contributing to European football events.

Venue

The Dublin Arena, or the Aviva Stadium, is a state-of-the-art sports facility that has earned recognition for its modern amenities and picturesque setting.

With a seating capacity to accommodate a large number of fans, the stadium provided an ideal backdrop for the culmination of the 53rd season of Europe's secondary competition.

Its significance as a venue for major sporting events, combined with its historical association with football, made it a fitting location for the Europa League final in 2024.

The stadium's rich history, including its previous role as the venue for the 2011 Europa League final, added an extra layer of significance to the 2024 final.

The Aviva Stadium's role as a hub for sporting excellence and memorable events further enhanced the appeal and grandeur of the Europa League final, elevating the overall experience for players, fans, and officials alike.

The 2024 final at the Dublin Arena was a testament to the stadium's enduring legacy and its ability to provide a world-class platform for the culmination of the Europa League campaign.

Its modern facilities, strategic location, and historical significance made it an ideal choice to host this prestigious event, reflecting the spirit of unity and competition that defines European football.

On the whole

The 2024 UEFA Europa League final at the Dublin Arena in Dublin, Ireland, was a culmination of months of intense competition, showcasing the best of European football talent and providing fans with a thrilling spectacle.

The date, time, and location of the final, coupled with the historical and cultural significance of the venue, added to the excitement and anticipation surrounding the event.
