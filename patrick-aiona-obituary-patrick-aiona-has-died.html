Patrick Aiona Obituary, Patrick Aiona Has Died

Publish date: 2024-08-22
0SHARESShareTweet

Patrick Aiona Obituary, Death Cause – Mr. Patrick Gabriel Aiona, Sr., age 71, of Hilo, Hawaii, passed away on July 8, 2023 in the Hilo Medical Center. He was born in Hilo, Hawaii on March 17, 1952 and was the President of Aiona Car Sales in Hilo. He was a member of the Hawaii Automobile Dealers Association (H.A.D.A.), Rotary Club of South Hilo, Portuguese Chamber of Commerce, Native Hawaiian Chamber of Commerce, Piihonua Homestead Community Association, Young Men’s Institute (Y.M.I.) and multiple Catholic Churches in East Hawaii. Visitation will be held at Dodo Mortuary Chapel in Hilo, Hawaii on July 21, 2023 (Friday) from 9:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.; memorial service will begin at 11:00 a.m.

Casual attire is requested. No Koden (monetary offerings). He is survived by wife, Annette Aiona of Hilo; sons, Patrick (Cyn Desha) Aiona, Jr. of Hilo and Shawn Aiona of Honolulu; daughter, Heather (Daniel Rhodes) Aiona of Hilo; siblings, Jane Aganus of Kamuela, Rosemarie Billie (Andy) Baclig of Hilo, Joanne Peralta of Washington State, William (Micci) Aiona of Kamuela, James (Carol) Aiona of Hilo, Cosie (Curtis) Sasaki of California and Mark (Tammy) Aiona of Kona; sisters-in-law.

Yolanda Aiona of Hilo and Myrna Aiona of Maui; grandchildren, Patrick Aiona, III of Hilo, Ava Aiona of Hilo, Kai Aiona-Agra of Hilo, Makena Aiona-Agra of Hilo and Moku Aiona-Rhodes of Hilo; an aunt, numerous nephews, nieces and cousins. It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Patrick Aiona, a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many. Patrick’s departure from this world leaves a void that will be deeply felt by all who had the privilege of knowing him. His legacy as a compassionate friend, devoted family member, and accomplished professional will forever be cherished and remembered.
