Blake Lively Posted a Throwback of Her Emmys Appearance with Leighton Meester

Publish date: 2024-07-08
  • Blake Lively took to Instagram to share some photos of her 2009 Emmys appearance.
  • She also included a picture of her and fellow Gossip Girl costar Leighton Meester as they presented an award onstage.

Hey Upper East Siders. Anyone in the mood for some nostalgia?

While the 71st Emmy Awards were in full swing last night, Blake Lively took to Instagram to post a sentimental throwback of her 61st Emmy Awards appearance in 2009. Then, she and Gossip Girl costar Leighton Meester dazzled the stage in Versace and Bottega Veneta dresses, respectively, as they presented the award for Outstanding Directing in a Comedy Series.

"10 years ago ✖️♥️✖️♥️," Lively wrote in the caption.

Husband Ryan Reynolds replied to her post with a typical cheeky comment, writing, "I can't believe how much I've changed."

If you miss seeing Lively and Meester making public appearances together, you can look forward to an upcoming Gossip Girl reboot, which will be streamed on HBO Max in the spring of 2020. While casting for the modernized series has yet to be announced, guest appearances from the original cast are definitely still open for consideration.

As reported by Entertainment Tonight, executive producer Josh Schwartz said at the Television Critics Association summer press tour, "We've reached out to all of them to let them know it was happening and we'd love for them to be involved if they want to be involved, but certainly didn't want to make it contingent upon [them being involved]."

Schwartz also told Us Weekly that the series will take place in the "same world" as the original 2000s show.
