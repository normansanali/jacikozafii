The Real Housewives of Dubai Star Caroline Stanbury Wiki | Bio

Caroline Stanbury (born on April 28, 1976) is a 46-years-old British influencer and brand ambassador who currently resides in Dubai. She started her career as a celebrity stylist and then sold her luxury products. In 2021 she married an ex-Madrid player and now lives in Dubai.

In 2022, her popularity reached heights after becoming part of a famous reality show titled “The Real Housewives of Dubai.” It is a Bravo TV show which showcases the life of rich wives living in Dubai. There are 5 more contestants in the show other than Caroline, namely Nina Ali, Chanel Ayan, Sara Al Madani, Caroline Brooks, and Lesa Milan Hall.

Profession & Net Worth

In 1994, Stanbury started her career as a stylist and soon became a famous personality among British movie stars. Within a couple of years, she became a celebrity in the British fashion industry. Caroline decided to launch her own products at the peak of her career, which proved supremely beneficial for her.

From London, she came to Dubai and where she started her career as a social media influencer, and big brands started approaching her. She also ran a podcast named “Divorced, Not Dead” to address a divorced woman’s problems.

Many a time, Caroline was called for interviews on different shows like “This Morning,” “E! News Live,” “Stay Home with Kris Fade & Friends,” and “Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen,” and “Ladies of London.”

Currently, she resides in a beautiful villa in Dubai and lives like a queen. The British celebrity has some expensive cars in her fleet like Rolls Royce Ghost, Porsche 911 Turbo, Range Rover Vogue, Mercedes S-Class, etc. Stanbury is said to have a net worth of USD 31.7 Million.

Wedding, Divorce, and Children

After coming to Dubai, Stanbury fell in love with a handsome guy named Cem Habib. After being in a romantic relationship for a couple of years, they both married in 2004. Caroline also gave birth to three kids, namely Yasmine, Zac, and Aaron. Everything was going great, but in 2019 they decided to split up and filed for divorce. Cem and Caro decided to maintain positive relations so that it won’t affect their kids.

After Cem, a ripped footballer named Sergio José Carrallo Pendás came into Stanbury’s life. Stanbury and Sergio have an age gap, but they decide to ignore it. In an interview with Mail Online, José revealed that her dad offered him a hefty amount to end her relationship with Caroline

Breaking all the odds, in 2021, they both got married. The wedding took place in Dubai at Raffles in Palm Resort. All of their family and friends were present there to witness their marriage.
