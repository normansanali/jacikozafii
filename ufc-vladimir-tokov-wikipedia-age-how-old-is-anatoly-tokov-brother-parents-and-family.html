Who Is Vladimir Tokov UFC? Age And Wiki

Vladimir Tokov is a professional Mixed Martial Artist.

As for his age, he is about 25. According to an Instagram post of his brother, Anatoly Tokov, on 3 October 2019, Vladimir was 22. Hence, he was born sometime in or about 1997 in Stary Oskol, Russia. However, the fighter himself hasn’t disclosed the details.

The lightweight stands tall at 5 feet and 10 inches and has a reach of 72 inches. Affiliated with Alexander Nevsky, he is a member of the FEDORTEAM.

Vladimir made his professional debut against Evgeniy Skakun in the Steel Battle 3 event on 29 July 2016. He won the bout via a first-round KO/TKO.

He then won four more fights before losing to Chris Gonzalez with a split decision on 21 August 2020 in the Bellator 224 event. He will be facing Daniele Scatizzi in the Bellator 275 event tomorrow, 25 February 2022.

According to Tapology, Vladimir had two amateur fights, of which he won against Sergey Petrov in the Cup Of Belgorod 2016 and lost against Vladimir Sergeev in the Cup Of Belgorod 2017.

As for his social media presence, he is active on Instagram as @tokov_vladimir.

Vladimir is yet to compete in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). However, he has fought in Russian Regional and Steel Battle Promotion.

Vladimir Tokov Parents And Family

As for Vladimir Tokov’s parents, their identities remain unknown.

The Russian fighter hasn’t disclosed much about them, including their profession. Nonetheless, we know that Vladimir has a brother named Anatoly Tokov.

His older brother is also a professional Mixed Martial Artist who has a record of 30 wins and 3 losses. According to Tapology, he is the current #48 in the Best MMA Middleweight Fighter list.

Similarly, Vladimir’s sister-in-law is Masha, @masha__tokova (Instagram). He also has two nieces whose identities are a mystery.

Talking about his family, he is married and has a daughter.
