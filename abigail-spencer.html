Biography

Abigail Spencer was born in Gulf Breeze, FL on August 4, 1981. The actress is best known for her role as Rebecca Tyree on All My Children and as professor Lucy Preston in NBC’s Timeless. She also starred in the science fiction action film Cowboys and Aliens. Other credits include The Haunting in Connecticut 2: Ghosts of Georgia and Oz the Great and Powerful. She appeared with Jon Hamm on several episodes of Mad Men. She traveled to Germany to sing with her church choir while a freshman in high school.
