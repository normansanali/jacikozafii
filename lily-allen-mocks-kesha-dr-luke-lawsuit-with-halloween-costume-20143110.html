Lily Allen Mocks Kesha, Dr. Luke Lawsuit With Halloween Costume

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Got beef? Lily Allen went for the controversial this year with her Halloween costume at Kate Hudson‘s annual Halloween party on Thursday, Oct. 30. The singer dressed as a gynecologist named “Dr. Luke.“

Wearing her version of teal doctor’s scrubs and a stethoscope around her neck, Allen, 29, pinned a name tag to her ensemble which read: “Dr. Luke, Gynecology Department.”

Allen’s costume is an obvious reference to the music producer’s ongoing legal battle with Kesha. As previously reported by Us Weekly, Kesha is suing Dr. Luke, claiming he sexually assaulted, drugged, and verbally abused her throughout her career. Luke, for his part, has filed a countersuit and denies the allegations.
