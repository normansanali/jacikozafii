First-Ever Match Announcement for 1/31 AEW Dynamite

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Tony Khan, the President and CEO of All Elite Wrestling, has confirmed an exciting match for this week’s episode of Dynamite. Jon Moxley is set to face Jeff Hardy in a first-time ever match, which adds to the anticipation of the show’s return of AEW Rankings.

Tony Khan promoted the match on Twitter, highlighting the significance of this bout and the return of the AEW Rankings. This match between Jon Moxley and Jeff Hardy promises to be a must-see moment for AEW fans.

In addition to the Moxley vs. Hardy match, here is the updated card for the upcoming episode of Dynamite, which will take place at the UNO Lakefront Arena in New Orleans, LA:

  • Jon Moxley vs. Jeff Hardy
  • Dealer’s Choice: Swerve Strickland vs. an opponent of Hangman Page’s choosing
  • Dealer’s Choice: Hangman Page vs. Toa Liona
  • Deonna Purrazzo vs. Taya Valkyrie (with Toni Storm on commentary)
  • Chris Jericho vs. Kyle Fletcher

With these exciting matchups, AEW fans have plenty to look forward to in the upcoming episode of Dynamite.
