Top 10 Most Famous Instagram Influencers of 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-06

In today's digital world, social media influencers have become an integral part of marketing strategies for brands and businesses.

Instagram, in particular, has been a breeding ground for these influential personalities, with their ability to engage and sway millions of followers.

1. Khaby Lame

Number of followers: 80M (@khaby00)
Genre: Comedy, Lifestyle
Where they got their start: TikTok

Khaby Lame, a Senegalese-Italian content creator, is the most-followed person on Instagram.

He rose to fame through TikTok and has successfully transitioned to Instagram, where he collaborates with NBA players, mingles with celebrities like Snoop Dogg, and promotes brands such as Binance, Google Pixel, and Hugo Boss.

2. Huda Beauty

Number of followers: 51.9M (@hudabeauty brand account) + 2.6M (@huda personal account)
Genre: Beauty
Where they got their start: YouTube

Huda Kattan, the face of Huda Beauty, has leveraged her beauty blog and YouTube channel to build a billion-dollar global beauty empire. Her Instagram feed features captivating "get ready with me" videos using her brand's products, making her a prominent figure in the beauty industry.

3. Lele Pons

Number of followers: 50.5M (@lelepons)
Genre: Entertainment
Where they got their start: Vine

Lele Pons, known for her Vine comedy, has diversified her portfolio with pursuits in music, modeling, and acting.

Her Instagram content showcases a blend of modeling shots, "day in the life" content, and endorsements for brands like the Sweat app.

4. Cristiano Ronaldo

Number of followers: 537M (@cristiano)
Genre: Sports
Where they got their start: Football

Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo tops the list of the most followed Instagram influencers. Apart from his athletic prowess, Ronaldo's brand partnerships with Nike, Herbalife, TAG Heuer, Jacob & Co. Jewelry, and Louis Vuitton have solidified his position as a social media powerhouse.

5. Lionel Messi

Number of followers: 422M (@leomessi)
Genre: Sports
Where they got their start: Football

Argentine footballer Lionel Messi boasts a massive Instagram following, making him one of the top influencers. His collaborations with brands like Adidas, Budweiser, Gatorade, Huawei, Pepsi, and Lay’s have further amplified his influence in the digital space.

6. Kylie Jenner

Number of followers: 378M (@kyliejenner)
Genre: Fashion, Lifestyle
Where they got their start: Reality TV

Kylie Jenner, a prominent figure in the fashion and lifestyle space, has amassed a substantial following on Instagram.

Not only does she have a mass following, but Kylie Jenner's Net Worth is another topic to explore as she is a great businessperson.

7. Selena Gomez

Number of followers: 372M (@selenagomez)
Genre: Music, Entertainment
Where they got their start: TV, Music

Selena Gomez, an accomplished singer and actress, has successfully transitioned her stardom into social media influence.

8. Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson

Number of followers: 359M (@therock)
Genre: Entertainment, Fitness
Where they got their start: Wrestling, Acting

Dwayne Johnson, known as "The Rock," has an extensive following on Instagram, showcasing his influence in the entertainment and fitness realms. With his engaging content and brand partnerships, including collaborations with Under Armour and VOSS Water, he continues to be a dominant force on the platform.

9. Nusr-Et Saltbae

Number of followers: 47.3M
Genre: Food, Lifestyle
Where they got their start: Culinary Arts

Nusr-Et Saltbae, a renowned figure in the culinary world, has captured the attention of millions on Instagram with his unique culinary skills.

10. Addison Rae

Number of followers: 40.1M
Genre: Dance, Entertainment
Where they got their start: TikTok

Addison Rae, known for her captivating dance videos, has garnered a substantial following on Instagram. Her influence extends to brand partnerships, making her a notable figure in the entertainment and dance space.

The top 10 most famous Instagram influencers of 2024 represent a diverse range of genres, from comedy and beauty to sports and entertainment.

Their ability to engage and influence millions of followers has made them pivotal figures in the realm of social media marketing.

As brands continue to leverage the persuasive power of influencers, these personalities play a crucial role in shaping consumer behavior and brand perceptions on Instagram.
