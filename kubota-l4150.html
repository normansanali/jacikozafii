Kubota L4150 Compact Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota F2302-D1-2 diesel 5-cylinder liquid-cooled 142 ci [2.3 L] engine, which delivers 50 hp [37.3 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota L4150 Compact Utility Tractor featured a Kubota F2302-D1-2 diesel 5-cylinder liquid-cooled 142 ci [2.3 L] engine, 12 qts [11.4 L] Oil capacity, 8 forward and reverse gears, 4×2 2WD, 4×4 MFWD 4WD, 3,525 lbs [1598 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 3750 to 4080 pounds weight. 

Kubota L4150 Compact Utility tractor attachments include a Kubota BF900 front-end loader and a Kubota L4540 backhoe.
