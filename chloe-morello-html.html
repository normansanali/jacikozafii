Chloe Morello - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Leading YouTube beauty and style guru in Australia who has earned over 2.6 million subscribers. She offers informative, educational, and fun tutorials as well as reviews on beauty and skincare products, hair styling advice, and tips on personal style.

Before Fame

She worked at her family's motel on the NSW coast of Australia and also used to work at a crematorium.

Trivia

Her videos have captivated people as far as The Netherlands and Saudi Arabia. She has worked on campaigns with top brands such as L'Oreal Paris, YSL, Napoleon Perdis, Vaseline, and Simone Perele and will be working with Priceline in the near future.

Family Life

She was raised with a younger sister named Ellie. Her father, an Italian, immigrated to Australia as a child. In January 2018, she married Sebastian Maximiliano. In June 2021, they welcomed their son Santino.

Associated With

She created videos profiling A-list actresses' Cannes Film Festival makeup looks. Some of her videos were for actresses including Eva Longoria, Blake Lively, and Barbara Palvin.
