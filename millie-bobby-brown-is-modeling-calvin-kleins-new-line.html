Millie Bobby Brown Is Modeling for Calvin Klein

Publish date: 2024-09-28

This morning, Raf Simons announced his first collection for Calvin Klein, WWD reports. The line is called Calvin Klein By Appointment and it's a 14-piece, made-to-measure collection.

"Calvin Klein By Appointment 1-14 is very much about celebrating the American woman and American fashion," Calvin Klein COO Raf Simons said on Instagram.

The company released several images from the line, each more captivating than the last.

But the one that grabbed our attention the most was of Calvin Klein's latest famous model, Stranger Things' Millie Bobby Brown.

"Calvin Klein is not only about iconic underwear and jeans—it's about so much more," Simons was quoted as saying in a tweet from the company.
