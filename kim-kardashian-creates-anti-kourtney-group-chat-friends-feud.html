Kourtney Kardashian Slams Sister Kim For Creating An Anti-Kourtney Group Chat With Her Friends

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Highlights

  • Kim and Kourtney Kardashian's feud continues as Kim reveals an anti-Kourtney group chat among her friends and family to vent about her behavior.
  • Kourtney accuses Kim of being a narcissist who cares only about herself and how she looks to the world.
  • Kim questions Kourtney's happiness in life, leading to a heated exchange where Kourtney expresses her desire to get away from her sisters.

The feud between Kourtney and Kim Kardashian continues, as Kim revealed she and her sister’s loved ones are in an anti-Kourtney group chat to express annoyance at her behavior.

During The Kardashians season four premiere, the sisters got into an explosive argument that had the both of them using choice words. At one point, Kim told Kourtney she’s not the only one who gets frustrated easily by her, claiming that Kourtney’s friends often get together via text to vent about her.

Kim Defended Creating A Group Chat To Gossip About Kourtney

"All of your friends call us complaining, whether you think they're the ones going to you, they're all coming to us on the side, saying the opposite to us, so we're all confused," Kim told claimed to Kourtney over a phone call.

Moreover, Kim revealed she’s in a group chat with Kourtney’s friends and other family members. "And we're on a group chat that's actually labeled 'Not Kourtney.'"

“[We] have to funnel what your friends are saying to us and have to figure why you're such a different person and why you have this vendetta out,” Kim explained of why they needed to create the group chat.

When Kourtney pressed her sister to know why she’d participate in a group chat that’s so hurtful, Kim defended herself. "They're your friends, the ones that you speak to on a daily basis,” the SKIMS founder said. “But you take it all out on me. And I'm trying the best that I can."

This prompted Kourtney to call her younger sister selfish. "It's about you. You are a narcissist," she clapped back. "Anything you do, it's about you and about how it looks to the world about you."

Kim Believes Kourtney Isn't Happy In Life

Kim went on to challenge her sister, asking her if she was happy in life, since those around her are beginning to question it. Kourtney is currently expecting a baby with her new husband Travis Barker. She already has three kids from her relationship with Scott Disick.

"We all are concerned,” Kim pressed. “We all think that you're just really not happy."

To this, Kourtney replied, "So, you're telling me that I'm unhappy and that I'm miserable and that you have a side chat called 'Not Kourtney,' but you're telling me that you're happy for me. I have a happy life and the happiness comes when I get the f--k away from you guys—specifically you."

Kourtney said it would make her feel better to see the group chat deleted, but Kim admitted she has chats like this for everyone she’s close to. "We have side chats about everybody. That's just what you do,” Kim said.

Kim and Kourtney have been feuding last year after Kim landed a partnership with Dolce & Gabana after they dressed Kourtney and Travis for their Italian wedding. Kourtney later accused Kim of turning her wedding into a networking and business opportunity, and they’ve seemed to be on the out ever since.
