Who is Marilyn Manson?

Marilyn Manson, born Brian Hugh Warner, is a figure synonymous with controversy and artistic provocation in the music world. Emerging in the early 1990s, Manson quickly became known for his shock-rock performances, dark and often controversial imagery, and theatrical stage presence.

His music, a fusion of industrial, metal, and alternative rock, along with his unique visual style, has garnered a dedicated fan base and significant media attention. Despite facing criticism and backlash, Manson has maintained a strong presence in the music industry, influencing many artists and genres.
