Glee's Heather Morris: How I Got My Amazing Body

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Known as much for her hilarious one-liners as she is for her incredible dance moves, Heather Morris has become one of Glee's breakout stars.

"I'm exactly like Brittany — I love to dance, I like to have fun, and I want to make everybody laugh," Heather Morris tells Women's Health of her onscreen alter-ego.

And yet, with her six-pack abs, lean arms and supertoned legs, Morris, 24, doesn't exactly look like most high school girls.

"The secret behind my fabulous legs? Eat a lot of Goldfish!" the actress laughs. "Do whatever you like to do, eat how you want to eat, but just make sure you stay active. I take a lot of exercise cardio classes; I do tons of squats."

Her fitness routine aside, Morris says her Glee castmates have become like family since the show premiered in 2009.

"The more storylines I have with certain characters the closer I get with them. I got a lot closer with Kevin McHale [who plays Artie] recently because we have a lot of scenes together; and Jenna Ushkowitz [who plays Tina], also."
