Who Did Tom Hiddleston Date Before Taylor Swift?

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Jessica Chastain: Though Chastain has been adamant about the fact that the two are just friends, romance rumors were sparked in 2013 when an insider close to the pair told The Daily Mail, "Tom is taken with Jess and it's serious.
