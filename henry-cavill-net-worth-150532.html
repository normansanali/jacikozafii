Publish date: 2024-09-02
Henry Cavill is an English actor born on May 5, 1983 in Jersey, England. He is best known for his roles in films such as The Count of Monte Cristo, Immortals, Stardust, and The Tudors, where he played Charles Brandon. He gained further recognition for his portrayal of Superman in the 2013 film Man of Steel, alongside Amy Adams.

About

Popular actor who has appeared in films like The Count of Monte Cristo, Immortals, and Stardust. He played Charles Brandon on The Tudors and was cast as Superman alongside Amy Adams in the 2013 film Man of Steel. 

Before Fame

His first acting experience came in school plays, and his first role on camera was as the lead in a minor movie called Laguna. He made his television debut as Chas Quiter in a 2002 episode of The Inspector Lynley Mysteries. 

Trivia

He played The Hunter in the 2006 film Red Riding Hood. 

Family Life

He dated Lucy Cork in 2017. He previously got engaged to Ellen Whitaker, but the couple separated before they got married. 

Associated With

He starred in Immortals with Freida Pinto.
