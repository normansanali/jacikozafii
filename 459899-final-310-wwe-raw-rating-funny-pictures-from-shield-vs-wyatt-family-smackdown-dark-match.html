3/10 WWE Raw Rating

the viewership for this week's episode of Monday Night Raw was down from the relatively high numbers since the Royal Rumble PPV in January. The final cable television rating for the 3/10 edition of WWE Raw was a 3.09, which is down from last week's rating and viewership, as well as one of the lower numbers of 2014 so far. This is with advertised appearances by Hulk Hogan and the Undertaker adding to the WWE's normal roster of Superstars.
