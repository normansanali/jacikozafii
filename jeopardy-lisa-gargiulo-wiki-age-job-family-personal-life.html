Publish date: 2024-09-11

Lisa Gargiulo is a teacher from Bryn Mawr, Delaware County, Pennsylvania. She is now making her new identity as a Jeopardy contestant to appear on the 39th season of the popular quiz game show.

Lisa Gargiulo (pictured with Jeopardy host Mayim Bialik) will appear as a contestant on ne Jeopardy episode on Wednesday, 31 May 2023. (Source: Lisa’s Facebook)

There, she will face two contestants; a returning champion Ilhana Redzovic, an investment associate from Chicago, Illinois, and Kyle Marshall, a library branch manager from Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. 

Ilhana ended the winning streak of two-day champion Diandra D’Alessio on Tuesday’s episode. She emerged as a new champion with a total score of $20,400.

What Is Lisa Gargiulo’s Job?

Gargiulo holds a job in the educational sector. She is a teacher at Hill Top Preparatory School in Bryn Mawr.

There, she teaches English in seventh and eighth grade and serves as the head of the school’s English Department. She is now in her 11th year at school, joining in 2012.

Besides, she is involved in advising in the Middle School Student Council and is touted as a key organizer of fun activities at the school for students and faculty, often trivia contests, so she can get practice in.

Lisa also serves as the program director of Camp Twin Creeks, a summer camp in West Virginia. Prior to that, she was a teacher at Clark County School District in Clark County, Nevada where she served from 2007 to 2012.
