Getting to Know Bella Poarch

Wondering who Bella Poarch is and looking for her complete profile details? Well, she is a well-known figure, and here is some information about her biography, wiki, height, weight, age, and net worth.

Bella Poarch, born on February 8, 1997, is a renowned Filipino-American personality known for her skills as a singer, musician, social media influencer, TikTok star, content creator, former US Navy veteran, and entrepreneur. Her rise to fame began with the viral spread of her adorable lip-sync TikTok videos. Notably, she holds the record for creating the most liked TikTok video, a lip-sync of “Soph Aspin Send” by British rapper Millie B.

Biography & Profile Summary

Bella Poarch, 26 years old as of 2023, was raised in a Christian family in San Fabian, Pangasinan, Philippines. She holds dual Filipino and American nationality and has a strong belief in the Christian religion.

Her educational journey started at a local high school in the Philippines before pursuing her graduation at a local private university in Texas, United States. Her childhood was scarred by physical and mental abuse from her father, but despite these challenges, she always aspired to have a military career while also nurturing her passion for music.

Family, Husband & Relationships

Bella Poarch’s father, Mr. Poarch, serves as a military officer in the US Army, while her mother, Mrs. Poarch, is a homemaker. She has three adoptive siblings, with her sister’s name known, while her brother’s identity remains undisclosed.

Regarding her marital life, Bella was married to Tyler Poarch in 2019. However, the couple went through a separation and eventually got divorced in 2022.

Height & Weight

Bella Poarch is a stunning lady with an attractive personality, a cute smile, and a beautiful, curvaceous figure. Her body measurements are approximately 28-23-35 inches, and she stands at around 5 feet 3 inches tall, weighing approximately 55 kg. She boasts long, shiny black hair and captivating dark brown eyes. Notably, she faced controversy in 2020 for getting a tattoo of the rising sun flag.

Career

In 2020, Bella Poarch started her career by gaining recognition on TikTok, where she initially showcased gaming and cosplay content. She captivated a substantial following, which led to her uploading popular lip-sync videos, including the viral sensation “Sophie Aspin Send” by Millie B. Her success on TikTok paved the way for her expansion onto other platforms like YouTube and Twitter.

Furthermore, she ventured into the fashion industry by launching a limited clothing line, RIPNDIP x Paca, and garnered attention from prominent Esports organizations. In 2021, she inked a record deal with Warner Records.
