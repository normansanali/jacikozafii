Vivienne Westwood Biography

Vivienne Westwood is best known as Fashion Designer, Businesswoman who has an estimated Net Worth of $50 Million. British fashion designer who popularized punk fashion and worked with musician Malcolm Mclaren to create the boutique SEX. She was born on April 8,1941, Derby, England. She has won the British Designer of the Year Award multiple times for her influence in fashion. She worked as a primary school teacher. The wedding dress worn by Sarah Jessica Parker in the Sex in the City film was designed by Westwood. She married Sam Westwood in 1962, with whom she had a son, and they divorced in 1965. She married Andreas Kronthaler in 1992. She has two sons named Joseph Corré and Ben Westwood. Her zodiac sign is Aries
