Jillian Barberie Splits With Husband Grant Reynolds

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Another one bites the dust. Spouses Jillian Barberie and Grant Reynolds are calling it quits, the former Good Day L.A. co-host tells Us Weekly in a statement.

"It is with great regret that my marriage to Grant is ending after more than six years," Barberie, 46, says. "He is a special man and we have two amazing children together Ruby, 5, and son Rocco, 3. This was a mutual decision that was not taken lightly and we are committed to our children and will work together to ensure their happy and healthy upbringing."

The couple's impending split was first reported by Us February 6. Barberie and Reynolds, an actor, wed in July 2006. The two appeared in Fox Reality Channel's Househusbands of Hollywood, which ran for one season in 2009. A source tells Us "the decision to split was mutual."

This will be the second divorce for Barberie, who was married to professional baseball player Bret Barberie from 1996 to 2002.
